Instructions

In this study, your task is to decode 5 digit numbers into corresponding letters. As shown in the example below, on the screen you will see a table which displays a correspondence between the letters and the numbers. At the bottom of the screen you will see a 5 digit number which is to be decoded into a sequence of 5 letters. To submit the sequence of letters you can either click “Submit” or press “Enter”.

For each correctly decoded 5 digit number you will receive {{C.piece_rate| to2}} GBP. Once you submitted a correct sequence of letters you will see a new screen with a new 5 digit number to be decoded and a new correspondence table. If you submit an incorrect sequence, you will see a pop-up and the entry field will become empty for the next attempt. The entry field is not case sensitive. You have {{C.round_time}} minutes to decode as many 5 digit numbers as you wish. The timer on top of the screen will indicate how much time remains and how many sequences you already decoded correctly.
